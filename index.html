About the Project

This project aims to develop sustainable, efficient logistics solutions for rural e-commerce, reducing CO2 emissions and improving service availability. It contributes to the UN's 2030 goals for sustainability.

Project Timeline

  • 2023-09-01: Project Start
  • 2023-12-01: WP1 Complete
  • 2024-03-01: WP2 Final Phase
  • 2024-06-01: WP3 Multi-Stakeholder Engagement

Work Packages

WP1: Rural Consumer Mapping

We surveyed and interviewed consumers to understand their needs and preferences for delivery methods.

WP2: Environmental Performance Modeling

Developing models to assess the environmental impact of different delivery methods and optimizing logistics systems.

WP3: Multi-Stakeholder Engagement

Engaging stakeholders to develop climate-neutral logistics solutions for rural areas.
